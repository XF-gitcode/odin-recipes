Creamy Beef Pasta

Description

Simple, but always delicious. One pot pasta is an easy to make dish that feeds the whole family, or even yourself with all the leftovers you'll have.

With a creamy tomato based sauce, your pasta of choice, ground beef and herbs, you'll make a pasta no one could turn their nose up from.

Ingredients

  • 500g~ beef mince
  • 2 cup chicken stock
  • 500g pasta shells
  • 3/4 cup cream
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1 large onion
  • 3 tbsp tomato pasta
  • 1 large canned tomato
  • 1 tbsp italian herbs
  • 1 tsp chilli flakes

Steps

  1. Coat large pot with oil, saute garlic and onion in oil, then add beef to cook.
  2. Add italian herbs, wait 30 seconds before adding tomato paste and stirring it in for a minute.
  3. Add chicken stock, canned tomato, chilli flakes and salt/pepper. Stir well before adding in the pasta shells.
  4. Once it comes to a simmer, let it cook for 15 minutes, until the pasta is cooked. Stir often and test the pasta to ensure it's cooked.
  5. Stir in the cream, letting it simmer again for another minute such that the pasta is well cooked.
  6. Take it off the stove, stir and serve in bowls. Add salt/pepper and parmasan cheese to taste.
