Thai Chicken Salad

Description

The delicious mix of flavours encompassing the South East Asian dish balance sweet, sour, savoury and even chilli create a delightful experience, while keeping you nourished and healthy.

A mixture of fried chicken breast, Thai chilli lime dressing, and plenty of veggies all come together for an easy to make meal for any time of day.

Ingredients

  • 200g~ chicken breast
  • 1 tbsp chopped coriander
  • 1.5 tbsp chilli garlic sauce
  • 1 garlic
  • 1.5 tbsp lime juice
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp canola oil
  • 1.5 tbsp sugar
  • 150g lettuce leaves
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes
  • 1 cucumber
  • 1/4 red onion
  • 1/2 red chilli
  • 1/2 cup roast cashew
  • 1/4 cup coriander

Steps

  1. Let chicken reach room temperature before cooking.
  2. Boil water in a pan, add chicken and let it simmer before taking it off heat for 20 minutes.
  3. Remove chicken from pan to shred and cool.
  4. Insert chopped coriander, chilli garlic sauce, minced garlic, fresh lime juice, soy sauce, canola oil, and sugar into a jar.
  5. Shake well and taste until well balanced.
  6. Place the lettuce leaves, halved cherry tomatoes, sliced cucumber, sliced red onion, sliced chilli (no seeds), roast cashews and coriander into a salad bowl.
  7. Mix with the shredded chicken and drizzle most the dressing over the salad to mix in.
